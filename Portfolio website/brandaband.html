Brand a band

This project focused on crafting a unique identity for the artist Robin van Zandt, aligning his personal style with a compelling brand presence. Through Brand a Band, we set out to translate his passion, personality, and musical essence into a visual and emotional experience that resonates with his audience. Robin’s vision was clear: a bold, modern aesthetic infused with a touch of raw energy and authenticity. By diving deep into his musical identity, we developed a cohesive brand strategy, including a striking logo, merchandise designs, and an engaging social media presence. Each element was designed to reflect his artistry and connect with his fans on a deeper level. The end result? A brand that captures the very essence of Robin van Zandt, making him not just an artist but a recognizable and relatable figure in the music scene. With this project, we didn’t just build a brand; we created a platform for Robin to grow and amplify his voice, staying true to who he is while reaching new heights.
