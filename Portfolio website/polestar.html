Polestar car rental project

This project focuses on promoting eco-friendly driving habits through a unique app and car interface. The design features a mobile app and an in-car display (CarPlay) that showcase a virtual plant reacting to the driver’s behavior. The plant thrives with responsible driving but deteriorates with reckless habits, providing visual feedback both on the phone and the car’s screen. The project includes Figma design mockups and a Kotlin-based prototype demonstrating the concept.
