Circus of dreams

This project was all about turning a personal vision into reality with Circus of Dreams, a hardstyle festival entirely designed and executed by me. As the organizer, DJ, and creative mind behind the event, I set out to create an unforgettable experience for fans of the genre. The festival featured three electrifying one-hour sets performed by me, showcasing a mix of high-energy tracks and personal favorites. The immersive atmosphere was brought to life with a dreamlike theme, blending vibrant visuals and pounding beats to captivate the audience. To capture the essence of the event, I produced a dynamic aftermovie highlighting the best moments—from the powerful drops to the euphoric crowd reactions. This video encapsulated the energy, passion, and creativity that went into making Circus of Dreams a reality. By combining my love for hardstyle with a hands-on approach to event creation, Circus of Dreams became more than just a project—it was a celebration of music, creativity, and the joy of sharing unforgettable moments with others.
